## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 Account data
- Email address
- Password (stored hashed, never in plain text)
- Unique user identifier (UUID)

### 2.2 Profile data
- Fitness goal (build muscle, lose fat, get stronger, cardio fitness)
- Experience level
- Weekly training frequency
- Available equipment (home, basic gym, full gym)
- Optional: first name, gender, age, body weight, height
- Preferred weight unit (kg / lb)

### 2.3 Workout data
- Workout sessions (date, duration, exercises performed)
- Sets logged (weight, reps, RPE — rate of perceived exertion)
- Optional notes
- Computed personal records and progression metrics

### 2.4 AI Coach interactions
- Messages you send to the AI Coach
- Contextual information shared with the AI (relevant subset of your profile and recent workout history)
- AI Coach responses

### 2.5 Subscription data
- Subscription status (Free, Pro)
- Subscription transaction identifiers, purchase date, renewal date
- Anonymous subscriber identifier used by our subscription provider

### 2.6 Technical data
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Anonymous device identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) — only if you grant tracking permission via your operating system's prompt
- Crash and error reports
- Approximate region inferred from IP address (not stored)

We do **not** collect: precise location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, biometric data, health/medical records, or payment card details (payment information is processed exclusively by Apple or Google).

## 7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** your personal data (Art. 15)
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data (Art. 16)
- **Erase** your data — right to be forgotten (Art. 17); also available via in-app "Delete account"
- **Restrict** processing (Art. 18)
- **Portability**: receive your data in a structured, commonly-used format (Art. 20)
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, where consent is the legal basis
- **Lodge a complaint** with the CNIL (French data protection authority — [cnil.fr](https://www.cnil.fr)) or your local supervisory authority

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@forge-ai.com. We respond within 30 days.

## 8. Children

Forge AI is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures:

- HTTPS encryption in transit
- Encryption at rest on our database
- Short-lived JWT-based authentication tokens
- Local encryption of sensitive data on your device (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore)
- Row-Level Security on every database table — you can only access your own data
- Regular dependency updates and security reviews

No system is 100% secure. If a personal data breach occurs that may put your rights and freedoms at risk, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours, as required by GDPR.

## 10. Cookies and tracking

The App does not use web cookies. We may use anonymous device identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) **only with your explicit consent**, granted via the operating system's tracking prompt. You can withdraw this consent at any time through your device's privacy settings.
